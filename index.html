My process

Over the years, I have developed a thorough process for the analysis, design, and creation of material being posted on the web. In my process, I take the following steps:

If a thing is worth doing, it is worth doing badly (G.K. Chesterton)

  1. Identify – to ensure that both the needs and the expectations of the client are met

  2. Investigate – in-depth research, exploration and examination of the client’s needs

  3. Integrate – determining the project’s place within the overall marketing strategy of the client

  4. Illustrate – draft and display and revise sample layouts and interfaces
